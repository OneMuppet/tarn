# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind, be constructive, assume good faith. tarn is a small project built in the
open — we want it to be a place where people are glad to show up.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and welcoming. Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully. "Here's a measurement that contradicts that"
  is great; condescension is not.
- Assume contributors are doing their best with the time they have.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory or demeaning comments, trolling,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct that would reasonably make
someone unwelcome.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, commits —
and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer at
**david@ampdlabs.io**. Reports will be handled discreetly. Maintainers may remove
comments, commits, and contributions that violate this code, and may ban repeat or
egregious offenders.

---

This is a lightweight adaptation of the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/). Be decent. 🤝
